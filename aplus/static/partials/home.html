Review Pittsburgh PSSA test scores

The Pennsylvania System of School Assessment, or PSSA, is the state test for 3rd through 8th graders. Testng takes place in the spring.

Scores for both PSSA tests and Keystone exams are rated “Advanced” (above grade level), “Profcient” (at grade level), “Basic” (not consistently at gradelevel), or “Below Basic” (below grade level).
